RevenueCat acts as our processor/service provider for subscription management and retains data as needed to perform these services and comply with legal obligations (e.g., fraud prevention, tax/audit requirements).

  • Legal obligations: to meet accounting, tax, and consumer-protection requirements applicable to in-app purchases.

We do not collect personal information directly. To the extent Apple and RevenueCat process data to enable purchases/subscriptions, the categories may include:
